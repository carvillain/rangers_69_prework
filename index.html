About Me

I am 29 years old, and originally from Michigan. Currently I live in Chicago's South Loop and love it!

I have two bachelor's degrees. I attended Grand Valley State University and graduated in 2014 with
a bachelor of science in Psychology. After working briefly in a manual labor job, I decided to go
back to school and obtained a bachelor of arts in History from Michigan State University in 2016.

Previous Employment

Most recently I worked for Quicken Loans as an underwriter. My career with Quicken Loans lasted
from 2017 through 2021 and provided a great opportunity for an entry into professional life after college.
In my tenure with Quicken Loans, I had the fortunate opportunity to hold three positions,
in different parts of the business:

Mortgage Loan Originator (Febuary 2017-December 2017)
I assisted clients in obtaining creative solutions for their financial needs utilizing a
refinance on their home.
Credit Consultant (December 2017-March 2021)
While employed as a credit consultant, I helped clients improve their credit scores to qualify
for or improve the pricing on a mortgage transaction.
Credit Underwriter (March 2021-July 2021)
As a credit underwriter, I reviewed loan applications to ensure they met guidelines and find
creative solutions if the loan application was not approved.

Other than my tenure with Quicken Loans, I also spent some time selling cars at a car dealership.
That job was quite fun because I was able to work with clients face to face and the other employees
were like minded individuals. These previous positions have helped me determine that I want to get
out of the finance and customer services industries, in favor of a more technical role and the unique
challenges that comes with it.
